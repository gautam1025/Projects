A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Graduated from Saint Joseph College in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu in 1954 with a degree in Physics A distinguished scientist and aerospace expert, he was responsible for developing India's first satellite launch vehicle, the SLV-3 Elected as the 11th president of India in 2002 and served until 2007 Promoted India's nuclear weapons program and devised a plan for economic growth through technological development. He was also known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contributions to developing cruise missiles like Agni and Prithvi.
